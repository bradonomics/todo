ToDo+

Welcome to ToDo+! For each task, enter the Task Name, how many days until it is due, the amount of minutes you need to complete the task, and the Urgency of the task. The urgency is on a scale from 1-3 with 1 being the most urgent.

Next, press "Add to Schedule" and continue adding tasks the same as the first. You will notice a chart on the right adding your tasks as you go. Once you are done adding tasks, press the button under the chart.

We will organize your chart based on when it is due and the urgency of it.

Finally, start working! You will see the arduino counting down for the first thing that you need to do. Good luck!
